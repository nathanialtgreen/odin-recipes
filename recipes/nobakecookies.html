No-Bake Cookies

Description

Above, you'll find a photo of no-bake cookies, popular with large families whose baking skills might be characterized as less than stellar.

Ingredients

Below, you'll find essential ingredients for creating no-bake cookies courtesy of AllRecipes.

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 3 cups quick cooking oats
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix together sugar, butter or margarine, and milk in a saucepan. Bring to a boil and boil for one minute, stirring constantly.
  2. Remove from heat and mix in cocoa, quick oatmeal and vanilla. Drop by spoonfuls on waxed paper.
