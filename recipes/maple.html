Maple Syrup

Description

Above, you'll find a photo of a northern staple in breakfast and desserts: Maple syrup. Whether in pancakes or waffles, you'll find syrup to be an invaluable part of your culinary repitoire.

Ingredients

Below, you'll find essential ingredients for creating maple syrup courtesy of AllRecipes.

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon maple flavored extract

Steps

  1. Bring the water, white sugar, and brown sugar to a boil in a saucespan over medium-high heat.
  2. Reduce heat to medium-low, and stir in the maple extract.
  3. Simmer 3 minutes longer.
